1. Introduction

Welcome to Lawlaw Delights, your premier destination for authentic Filipino seafood delicacies from Bulan. Our platform connects you with local sellers offering traditional delicacies, complete with recipes to help you enjoy these treats at home.

Key Features

  • Product Marketplace - Browse and purchase authentic Filipino delicacies
  • Recipe Collection - Discover cooking recipes with step-by-step instructions
  • Real-time Chat - Communicate directly with sellers
  • Order Tracking - Track your orders from purchase to delivery
  • Review System - Share your experiences and read others' feedback

2. Getting Started

2.1 Creating an Account

  1. Navigate to the Register page by clicking "Sign Up" or visiting /register
  2. Fill in the required information:
    • Full Name - Your display name on the platform
    • Email Address - A valid email for verification and notifications
    • Password - Create a secure password
  3. Read and accept the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy
  4. Click Register to create your account
  5. You can also sign up using Google OAuth for faster registration

2.2 Email Verification

After registration, you must verify your email:

  1. Check your email inbox for a verification code (OTP)
  2. Enter the 6-digit OTP code on the verification page
  3. If you didn't receive the code, click Resend Code
  4. Once verified, you'll have full access to your account
Note: Unverified accounts have limited access. Complete verification to unlock all features.

2.3 Logging In

  1. Go to the Login page (/login)
  2. Enter your registered email and password
  3. Alternatively, use Sign in with Google
  4. Click Login to access your account

2.4 Password Recovery

Forgot your password? Here's how to reset it:

  1. Click Forgot Password on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Log in with your new credentials

3. User Guide (Buyers)

3.1 Browsing Products

Homepage (/)

  • View Featured Products - Curated selections by our team
  • Explore Best Sellers - Highest-rated products
  • Check New Arrivals - Recently added items

Products Page (/products)

  • Browse the complete product catalog
  • Use filters to narrow your search
  • View product cards with:
    • Product image
    • Name and price
    • Average rating
    • Stock status (In Stock / Out of Stock / Low Stock)

3.2 Viewing Product Details

Click on any product to see:

  • Product Images - High-quality photos
  • Description - Detailed product information
  • Price - Current selling price
  • Stock Level - Available quantity
  • Seller Information - Who's selling this product
  • Customer Reviews - Ratings and feedback from buyers
  • Add to Cart - Button to add to your shopping cart
  • Buy Now - Quick checkout option
  • Chat with Seller - Start a conversation

3.3 Shopping Cart

Adding Items:

  1. Click Add to Cart on any product
  2. Select the desired quantity
  3. Confirm the addition

Managing Your Cart (/cart):

  • View all items in your cart
  • Adjust quantities using +/- buttons
  • Remove items by clicking the delete icon
  • See subtotal for each item
  • View cart total at the bottom
  • Click Proceed to Checkout when ready
Note: You cannot purchase products from your own shop if you're a seller.

3.4 Checkout Process

  1. Review Cart - Verify items and quantities
  2. Select Address - Choose from saved addresses or add a new one
  3. Choose Payment Method:
    • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Pay when you receive your order
    • GCash - Mobile wallet payment
    • Credit/Debit Card - Online card payment
  4. Review Order - Final confirmation of all details
  5. Place Order - Complete your purchase

3.5 Managing Orders

Access your orders at Orders (/orders):

Order Status Types:

Status Description
Pending Order received, awaiting processing
Processing Seller is preparing your order
Shipped Order is on the way
Delivered Order has been delivered
Cancelled Order was cancelled

Order Details (/orders/[id]):

  • Complete order information
  • List of items purchased
  • Shipping address
  • Payment method used
  • Order timeline
  • Tracking information (when shipped)

3.6 Order Tracking

For shipped orders:

  1. Go to your order details
  2. Find the Tracking Number
  3. View Tracking History - Complete timeline of your order
  4. See Estimated Delivery Date
  5. Track real-time order status updates

3.7 Writing Reviews

After receiving your order:

  1. Go to the product page
  2. Scroll to the Reviews section
  3. Click Write a Review
  4. Select your rating (1-5 stars)
  5. Write your feedback
  6. Submit your review
Tip: Honest reviews help other buyers and sellers improve their products!

Click on a recipe to see:

4.4 Creating Your Own Recipes

Share your culinary creations with the community:

  1. Go to Add Recipe (/add-recipe)
  2. Fill in the recipe details:

    • Description - Brief overview
    • Difficulty Level - Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced
    • Prep Time & Cook Time
    • Servings
    • Recipe Image - Upload an appetizing photo
  3. Add Ingredients:
    • Click "Add Ingredient"
    • Enter quantity and ingredient name
    • Repeat for all ingredients
  4. Add Instructions:
    • Enter each step in order

7. Seller Guide

7.1 Becoming a Seller

Want to sell on Lawlaw Delights? Here's how:

  1. Go to Seller Application (/seller-application)

9. Security Features

Lawlaw Delights prioritizes your security:

Account Security

  • Password Hashing - Secure bcrypt encryption
  • Email Verification - OTP-based account verification
  • JWT Sessions - Secure token authentication

Session Protection

  • 30-Minute Timeout - Automatic logout after inactivity
  • 2-Minute Warning - Countdown alert before logout
  • Activity Monitoring - Tracks mouse, keyboard, and scroll activity

Data Protection

  • CSRF Protection - Prevents cross-site attacks
  • SQL Injection Prevention - Secure database queries
  • XSS Protection - Sanitized content rendering
  • Role-Based Access - Appropriate permissions for each user type

Best Practices

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Don't share your login credentials
  • Log out when using shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

10. Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common Issues

Q: I didn't receive my verification email

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Click "Resend Code" on the verification page
  • Ensure you entered the correct email

Q: I can't log in to my account

  • Verify you're using the correct email
  • Try the "Forgot Password" option
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies

Q: My order status isn't updating

  • Refresh the page
  • Check your internet connection
  • Contact the seller via chat

Q: I can't upload images

  • Ensure file size is under 2MB
  • Use supported formats (JPG, PNG)
  • Try a different browser

Q: Chat messages aren't sending

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page
  • Try logging out and back in

Account Issues

Q: How do I delete my account?

  • Go to Settings > Delete Account
  • Confirm your decision
  • Your data will be permanently removed

Q: Can I change my email address?

  • Contact support for email changes
  • Email changes require verification

Q: How do I become a seller?

  • Submit a seller application
  • Wait for admin approval
  • Access will be granted upon approval
